About Us


AR Women Wellness Ayurveda Centre, is one of Malaysia’s premier ayurvedic centre located in Sitiawan, Perak.

The main objective of our centre is to create awareness on Ayurvedic Lifestyle and it’s healthy possibilities among Malaysian Women and to provide quality oriented Ayurvedic Consultation and treatments by well educated and trained Ayurvedic Professionals from India at mere affordable way. Our centre offers a complete range of Ayurvedic treatment including intensive oil therapies for all types of healing without any modification (exactly as practiced in Kerala).

AR Women Wellness believes that Ayurvedic treatment is profoundly essential in achieving balance in Body & Mind, as well as in promoting long life.
